Athletics

Lincoln University Athletics and Cross Country Club is a great club for everyone. The club is for beginners, intermediate and advanced runners. The club has weekly socials and we train multiple times a week. We aim to build up member's fitness levels so that they can compete, if they wish, in various competitions such as British Universities & College Sport (BUCS) and the Lincoln 10k.

Football

The football club is one of the oldest clubs here at the University of Lincoln. The club has three teams, which play within the BUCS leagues on Wednesday afternoons. They are a vital part of the sports and societies office and most of the teams are highly ranked within the BUCS leagues playing against the best universities in the country. As well as regular post-match nights out the club hosts a number of social events throughout the year.

Computer Science

We are the Computer Science Society. We hold weekly meetings covering topics such as Linux, Python and Discord Botting. we also run larger events like Game Jams and Hachathons. If you are interested in computers, we are the society for you!

Comedy

The Comedy Society is a relaxed and friendly society for people who want to get involved in stand-up and other forms of comedy. In our weekly meetings we talk about comedy, rip-off panel shows, and work on our stand-up routines. We also host free comedy gigs, which anyone is welcome to attend. Our current meeting time and location is 6:30 every Tuesday in MB2602.
